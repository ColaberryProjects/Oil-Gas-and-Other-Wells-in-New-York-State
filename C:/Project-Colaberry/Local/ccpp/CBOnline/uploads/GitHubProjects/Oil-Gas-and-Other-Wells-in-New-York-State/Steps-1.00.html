Introduction 1
Abandoned wells are unplugged wells (primarily oil or gas wells) that have not been operated and maintained in accordance with prevailing statutes and regulations. Many abandoned wells have fallen into advanced states of disrepair. Orphaned wells are a subset of abandoned wells; they are unplugged wells that have been abandoned by their original owners or operators, and for which no legally responsible owner or operator can be identified.
